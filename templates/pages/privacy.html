Privacy Notice

Last updated: January 2025

What data we collect

  • Account data: your email address and username, collected when you register.
  • Event content: titles, descriptions, images, dates, and venue information you submit.
  • Server logs: standard web server logs (IP address, browser, pages visited) retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.

Why we collect it

  • To allow you to submit and manage dance events.
  • To display approved events publicly on the calendar.
  • To send password-reset emails when you request them.
  • To protect the platform from abuse (brute-force protection via IP logging).

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use analytics cookies or third-party tracking.
  • We do not display your name or email on public event listings — only the event content you choose to share.

Data retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, your personal data (email, username) is permanently removed. Events you submitted will remain on the calendar but will no longer be attributed to you.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To delete your account and anonymise your submitted events, use the account deletion page. For other requests, contact us at the email below.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, email privacy@cphdance.example.com .
