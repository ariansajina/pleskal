Subscribe

Stay updated — directly in your calendar.

You can automatically stay updated with events in pleskal by subscribing to "feeds". These are data sources that can be added to your calendar by copy-pasting their URL into the Import function of your calendar. By subscribing to feeds, you will see pleskal events directly in your calendar as a layer that can be switched on and off. New events will automatically be pulled in, but changes to existing events will also be visible — for instance date, time, description and location.

How often to update? I recommend that you configure your calendar app to auto-refresh every week or every day, but not more frequently than that.
