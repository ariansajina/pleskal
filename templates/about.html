About

pleskal is a public online calendar for dance and performance art events in Copenhagen. It aims to:

  • Help more people engage with the scene without having to track multiple websites, newsletters, and social media feeds.
  • Provide artists with an independent platform that allows equal visibility regardless of institutional affiliation or production size.
  • Promote community organising and autonomy within the scene.
  • Contribute to building a new digital commons, free(er) from technofeudalism.

To this end, pleskal follows a few guiding principles:

  • Smaller is better: intentionally hyperlocal and niche; no ambition to scale or become a multi-purpose app.
  • Communal curation: only members can submit events, any member can invite new members; a shared responsibility to negotiate access and curation.
  • Free and open source: the web page and the code are accessible to anyone.
  • Pomalo: take it easy. This page was built playfully at leisure. Issues will come up and be addressed, but maybe I'll frolic in the field first.

pleskal is inspired by Duk Op and Gancio.

For questions or to get in touch, reach out at: hello.pleskal@proton.me
